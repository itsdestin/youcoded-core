What is this?

A tool to remember everything that matters.

Claude Code is an AI tool developed by Anthropic. However, this AI tool is nothing like the ChatGPT or Gemini you might be familiar with. Claude can create every type of file, search the web, open any app, and navigate your screen if you desire. Destin's modifications turn all of that into a personal system that helps you stay on top of the things you care about — without the overhead of actually organizing them yourself.

Snap a screenshot of a flyer for an event next month, and it lands on your calendar. Forward yourself a rambling voice memo and the action items get pulled out. Journal about your day to decompress, and YouCoded quietly picks up on the rest — a goal you mentioned making progress on, a dentist appointment you need to schedule, an overdue assignment worth surfacing before you close the laptop. You just talk. The structure happens on its own.

Over time, it builds a living record of your life — the kind of thing you'd never sit down and write yourself, but wish you had. Who you met at that conference two years ago. What your friend's kid's name is. The argument that changed your mind about something. The details that make you you, preserved so you can actually find them when they matter most.

Nothing happens without your permission. YouCoded will always ask before taking any action.

Get started

Step 1: Run the installer

Open Terminal: Press Cmd + Space to open Spotlight, type Terminal, and press Enter. A window with a blinking cursor will appear.

Paste this and press Enter:

$ curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/itsdestin/youcoded-core/master/bootstrap/install.sh -o /tmp/install.sh && bash /tmp/install.sh

Open Windows Terminal: Press the Windows key, type Terminal, and press Enter. A window with a blinking cursor will appear.

Paste this and press Enter:

> powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -c "iwr -useb https://raw.githubusercontent.com/itsdestin/youcoded-core/master/bootstrap/install.ps1 -OutFile install.ps1; .\install.ps1"

Step 2: Talk to YouCoded

In that same terminal window, type:

$ claude

Then, press Enter. YouCoded will open. Type:

> /setup-wizard

YouCoded will walk you through the rest — choosing what to install, setting up your preferences, linking your external accounts, and making sure everything works.

WARNING: You may see lots of red "error" text and be asked for a multitude of confirmations while YouCoded completes this process. Don't worry; this is to be expected. Trust YouCoded (he is all-knowing) and approve any prompts.

If you'd like to know more, review the additional documentation at the bottom of this page.

◆

Core

The foundation. Session hooks, git sync, write guards, a specs system for documenting features, persistent memory, and commands for updates and contributions.

★

Life

A personal knowledge system. Conversational journaling, a living encyclopedia that YouCoded maintains about you, and automatic Google Drive backup.

⚡

Productivity

Task management and communication. Process your Todoist inbox, build custom skills, and read or send text messages through YouCoded.

❖

Modules

Optional add-ons for specific domains. Install additional skills tailored to your work or interests.

Each layer is independent. Life and Productivity both build on Core, but don't depend on each other. Want the technical details?
