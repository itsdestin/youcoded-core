# What is Claude Code?

You've probably chatted with Claude before — you open a browser, type a question, and get a response. Claude Code is that same Claude, but instead of living in a browser tab, it lives on your computer.

That might not sound like a big deal, but it changes everything.

## More Than a Chatbot

When Claude lives on your computer, it can do things. Real things. It can read your files, create new ones, manage your tasks, remember things about you across conversations, and even run programs. It's less like a chatbot and more like a personal assistant who actually has access to your stuff.

You talk to Claude Code in a **terminal** — that black window with text that you've probably seen in movies about hackers. Don't worry, it's not scary. You type in plain English, just like you would in a chat. Claude understands you the same way it always does.

## What's a Skill?

Think of a skill like an app for Claude. Each skill teaches Claude how to do something specific.

For example:
- A **journaling skill** teaches Claude how to help you write daily journal entries, ask good follow-up questions, and organize everything by date
- An **encyclopedia skill** teaches Claude how to build and maintain a detailed biography of your life — people you know, things you've done, what you believe in
- A **task management skill** teaches Claude how to work with your to-do list, process inbox notes, and keep you organized

Without skills, Claude Code is smart but generic. With the right skills installed, it becomes *yours* — tuned to your life, your workflows, your preferences.

## What's a Hook?

Hooks are things that happen automatically. You don't have to think about them.

For example:
- When you start a conversation, a hook might automatically sync your latest notes from the cloud
- After Claude edits a file, a hook might check that nothing sensitive was accidentally included
- A hook might update your status bar to show how much of Claude's memory you've used

Hooks run in the background. They're the invisible assistants that keep everything running smoothly.

## What This Toolkit Adds

ClaudifestDestiny is a collection of skills, hooks, and tools that transform Claude Code from a general-purpose assistant into a personal knowledge system. Here's what it includes:

- **Journaling** — A conversational journaling system that helps you document your life
- **Encyclopedia** — A living biography that Claude maintains about you, your relationships, and your history
- **Task Management** — Integration with Todoist, inbox processing, and automated capture
- **Text Messaging** — Read and send texts through Claude (requires additional setup)
- **Specialized Tools** — Domain-specific modules you can add based on your needs

You pick what you want. Install just the core foundation, add the personal knowledge system, include productivity tools, or go all-in. It's modular — take what's useful, leave what isn't.

## Do I Need to Know How to Code?

No. The toolkit is designed for people who don't code. The installer handles the technical setup, and after that, you just talk to Claude in plain English. If something goes wrong, Claude can usually fix it for you.

That said, if you *do* know how to code, there's a lot more you can do — create your own skills, customize hooks, build modules. The architecture documentation covers all of that.
