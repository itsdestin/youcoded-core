What is this?

Claude Code, but it's actually an app.

Claude Code is an AI tool developed by Anthropic. However, this AI tool is nothing like the ChatGPT or Gemini you might be familiar with. Claude can create every type of file, search the web, open any app, and navigate your screen if you desire. DestinCode puts all of that into a real app with a chat interface, custom themes, a skill marketplace, and even multiplayer games you can play while Claude works.

With the optional DestinClaude toolkit add-on, it goes even further. Snap a screenshot of a flyer for an event next month, and it lands on your calendar. Forward yourself a rambling voice memo and the action items get pulled out. Journal about your day to decompress, and DestinClaude quietly picks up on the rest — a goal you mentioned making progress on, a dentist appointment you need to schedule, an overdue assignment worth surfacing before you close the laptop. You just talk. The structure happens on its own.

Over time, it builds a living record of your life — the kind of thing you'd never sit down and write yourself, but wish you had. Who you met at that conference two years ago. What your friend's kid's name is. The argument that changed your mind about something. The details that make you you, preserved so you can actually find them when they matter most.

Nothing happens without your permission. DestinCode will always ask before taking any action.

FAQ

Everything DestinCode and its toolkit create — journal entries, your Encyclopedia, tasks — is stored in your own Google Drive or iCloud account. Nothing is sent to third-party servers beyond the services you explicitly connect. Your data stays yours.
The $20 goes to Anthropic for a Claude Pro subscription, which gives you access to Claude Code — the AI that powers everything. DestinCode itself is free and open source. You're paying for the AI, not the app.
Windows, macOS, Linux, and Android are all fully supported with native apps. You can also access DestinCode remotely from any web browser. Apple ecosystem integrations (iMessage, Apple Notes, etc.) are only available on macOS.
Not at all. DestinCode was built entirely by a non-developer using Claude Code itself. The app is designed for everyone — students, professionals, anyone who uses AI regularly. If you can send a text message, you can use DestinCode.
Yes — just describe the vibe you want and Claude builds it. Use /theme-builder inside the app. Themes support wallpapers, glassmorphism, particle effects, mascots, custom icons, and more. You can share your themes with friends or publish them to the community marketplace.
